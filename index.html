Hoofbeats in the West

Without horses we would not live in the world we do today. Horses have not only shaped the world of our imagination, but also the very roads on which we drive to work were 9 times of 10 were first marked by hoofprints.

In the west the prints of their hooves are as indelible as those of the awesome Bison, their manes flying into the wind and alert eyes, iconic as the American Eagle.

Yes, times have changed and they can never be the same, but the importance of giving these magestic beasts, whose history is so entwined with ours, a place in the country an honored place in our ecosystem can not be understated.
